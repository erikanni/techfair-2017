About Me

Forrest Mims III

Amateur Scientist and Electronics Popularizer

Forrest Mims III is an amateur scientist that has had many achievements like helping a weather company make their information correct after it randomly started going off course. He also made many simple projects with the 555 microchip, like timers, sound synthesizers, and others. He mainly encourages amateur science, which includes simple projects with electronics that just about anybody can make. He also encourages “citizen science” which means that anybody can go out to their local park and just simply observe and measure things and write them down. He also wrote many books on this topic, which were sold at RadioShack and started getting thousands of people interested. The case with most people was that those books were the reason that they got a job. Forrest Mims has written about 75 books, which got millions of copies sold. These books were mainly “engineer’s mini-notebooks” which had simple diagrams of different circuits and projects. Among these was my project, the Atari Punk console.
