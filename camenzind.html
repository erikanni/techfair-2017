Facts about the 555 timer IC

  • Over a billion produced each year
  • Invented in 1971
  • Consists of 25 transistors, 2 diodes, and 15 resistors
  • Has three operating modes: Bistable, Astable, and Monostable
  • Can be used to provide time delays, as an oscillator, and as a flip-flop element
